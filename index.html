Yisu Li 李一粟

yl46273@my.utexas.edu

I am a third-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I also spent a wonderful spring and summer as an exchange student at The University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin).

My research interests lie in multimodal learning, visual reasoning, and embodied AI. At CUHK, I worked on the visual reasoning capabilities of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. At UT Austin, I worked in the RPL Lab with Prof. Yuke Zhu and my research focused on transfer learning from human videos to humanoid robots and memory mechanisms for robots.
