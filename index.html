Yisu Li 李一粟

yl46273@my.utexas.edu

I am a third-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I also spent a wonderful spring and summer at The University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin).

My research interests lie in visual reasoning and robot learning. At CUHK, I worked on the visual reasoning capabilities of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. At UT Austin, I worked in the RPL Lab with Prof. Yuke Zhu and my research focused on transfer learning from human videos to humanoid robots and memory mechanisms for robot manipulation.
